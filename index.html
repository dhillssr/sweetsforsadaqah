Our Story

A small kitchen with a wide intention.

Sweets for Sadaqah began in a home kitchen with a simple question: what if a bakery existed not for itself, but for the people it could uplift? Each recipe is developed slowly, tested in small batches, and finished by hand — because what we make is more than a cookie. It is an offering, a reminder, and an invitation to give.

Honest ingredients

Real butter, unbleached flour, fair-trade chocolate. No shortcuts.

Half given back

Fifty percent of every order funds verified charitable causes.

Our Mission

Sweets for Sadaqah is rooted in giving. Inspired by the words of Allah SWT in Surah Al-Layl, our mission is to give with sincerity, remain mindful of Him, and trust in His promise of ease. Half of all proceeds are dedicated to charitable causes as an act of sadaqah. Every purchase is an opportunity to give sincerely, to uplift those in need, and to be counted among those who give and are mindful. Through every sweet enjoyed, we strive to turn ordinary moments into acts of ibadah, barakah, and collective reward for everyone who supports this mission.
